IMPORTANT — Before building:

Place your google-services.json file in THIS folder:
  android/app/google-services.json

To get it:
1. Go to console.firebase.google.com
2. Open your project
3. Click the gear icon → Project Settings
4. Under "Your apps" → Android app
5. If no Android app exists, click "Add app"
   - Package name: com.humanrhythms.app
   - App nickname: Human Rhythms
6. Download google-services.json
7. Place it here (android/app/)

Then in Codemagic, add google-services.json as an Environment File Variable
so it's never committed to GitHub.
